Job Application Form

definition:

A job application is a formal document or online form that job seekers submit
to apply for a job with an organization. It's a structured way for applicants
to provide personal, educational, and professional information to potential
employers.

      Job applications typically ask for details such as:

    • Contact information
    • Work history
    • Education
    • Skills
    • References
    • Other information relevant to the job opening
